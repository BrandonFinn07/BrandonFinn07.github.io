Welcome to the Games NI Website

Games NI is one of Northern Irelands largest membership organization, serving a range of companies that promote their content, we also provide the most up to date gaming news and events. We aim to advance the brands that we represent and enhance the lives of game developers across the whole of Northern Ireland.

About Us

Here is some information about the work we do.

Our aim

We have partnered with a range of companies such as Hypixel, Outsider Games and many other local gaming businesses in an aim to promote their most recent content. Companies who we represent have seen a significant boost in sales through having their work advertised on our social media pages and pride ourselves on having a website to is easy to navigate through and is rich with creators material. Our overall mission throughout this whole process has been to support and empower game developers all over Belfast to achieve fulfilling and sustainable careers going forward.We partner with a range of companies both large and small across the whole of Northern Ireland, in a hope we can boost their name and help them achieve new heights. We as a company are always looking for new ways to grow and adapt. We represent local developers, puslishers and service providers on the world stage, travelling to shows and conventions to show the world the huge skills of this wee country.

Latest News

Here is the latest news in the gaming world

Open Now!

Hypixel have recently opened a studio in Derry, which will create 50+ jobs for new or excisting game developers. These past years have been fantastic for the Northern Ireland games industy, and this news just adds to it. Some of the jobs available are a games developer, 3D artist, animator and a level designer. This recent acquisition comes after the news that Hypixel have released a new sandbox game titled Hytale. Due to the Covid-19 crisis, some of the plans and move have been delayed.

Out Soon!

Outsider Games are about to releas their Latest point & click game entitled Jennifer wilde. Jennifer Wilde was previously uploaded to Kickstarter in an attempt to gain enough funds to fully produce. This has successfuly been accomplished and is now located on Steam. Jennifer Wilde is targeting a 2021 release, and if everything goes to plan we should get our hands on game sometime next year.
